Commissions

PERFECT FOR SPECIAL OCCASIONS LIKE WEDDINGS, ANNIVERSARIES, BIRTHDAYS, BIRTHS, GRADUATIONS AND MORE ...

 

YOU CAN INCLUDE ANYTHING YOU LIKE IN YOUR CUSTOM PAPERCUT, THE POSSIBILITIES ARE ENDLESS! IF YOU'RE STRUGGLING FOR IDEAS WE LIKE TO CONSIDER THINGS SUCH AS; NAMES, DATES, PLACES, JOBS, HOLIDAYS, PETS, HOBBIES, INTERESTS, AND THINGS THAT ARE MEANINGFUL TO THE RECIPIENT.

 

ONCE YOU'VE ORDERED A CUSTOM PAPERCUT ON OUR SHOP, AND MESSAGED US WITH THE DETAILS OF WHAT YOU WOULD LIKE TO BE INCLUDED, WE'LL START DRAWING OUT THE DESIGN, AND SEND YOU A PROOF FOR YOU TO APPROVE. THIS GIVES YOU AN OPPORTUNITY TO AMEND ANY MISTAKES IN DATES, SPELLINGS, OR MAKE ANY CHANGES TO THE DESIGN. ONCE YOU'VE APPROVED THE DESIGN, WE WILL CUT IT BY HAND, (WHICH CAN TAKE 10+ HOURS DEPENDING ON THE DESIGN!) THEN POST IT OUT TO YOU AS SOON AS POSSIBLE

 

PLEASE LET US KNOW IF YOU HAVE ANY TIME CONSTRAINTS AND WE WILL DO OUR BEST TO ACCOMMODATE YOU.
