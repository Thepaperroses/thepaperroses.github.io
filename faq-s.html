Delivery

HOW LONG DO THE PAPERCUTS TAKE TO MAKE?

EACH PAPERCUT IS MADE BY HAND, AND DEPENDING ON THE DESIGN CAN TAKE ANYWHERE BETWEEN 1HR TO 30+ HRS. THE LARGER OR MORE DETAILED PAPERCUTS TAKE LONGER TO CREATE.

 

HOW LONG WILL MY ORDER TAKE TO ARRIVE ONCE ITS BEEN DISPATCHED?

WE POST ALL OF OUR ORDERS WITH THE ROYAL MAIL FIRST CLASS, HOWEVER WE CANT GUARANTEE  THE POSTAGE TIMES OF THE ROYAL MAIL, ESPECIALLY AT BUSY PERIODS. IF YOU NEED YOUR ORDER BY A SPECIFIC TIME, PLEASE DO LET US KNOW SO WE CAN CONSIDER OTHER OPTIONS.

 

ARE THE PAPERCUTS SENT WITH TRACKED DELIVERY?

MOST OF OUR ITEMS ARE SENT FIRST CLASS SIGNED FOR, TO KEEP THE POSTAGE COST DOWN FOR YOU. HOWEVER IF YOU WOULD LIKE TO PAY EXTRA FOR TRACKED DELIVERY, YOU CAN CHOOSE TO UPGRADE AT THE CHECKOUT.
