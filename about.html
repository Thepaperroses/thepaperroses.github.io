About

Hello, welcome to The Paper Roses

 

We're Laura & Victoria, paper artists and illustrators, and the co-owners of The Paper Roses. We are twins that live in Yorkshire, England, with our dog ( and not very helpful studio assistant) Harley.

 

We started The Paper Roses after graduating University with a degree in Surface Design for Fashion and Interiors, which is where we discovered paper cutting and decided we might have found something we're actually good at!

 

We called our shop The Paper Roses because our shop couldn't exist without paper, and our emblem here in Yorkshire is the Rose, so we put the two together and The Paper Roses was born!

 

All of our papercuts are hand drawn and crafted by us, they do take hours to cut, but we just love the process, and how more of the design appears with each cut. We find our inspiration everywhere, but we particularly love nature and botanical's.

 

We love creating custom orders that are perfectly unique, so let us know if you'd like us to make something just for you!
